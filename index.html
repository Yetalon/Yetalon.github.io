Sparking The Fire Podcast

The journey of young fighters in a dnd campaign

The podcast is going to be a DnD podcast that will have multiple seasons with varying ways of playing DnD. For our first season, we will be starting in a natural DnD setting. However, it will still have a twist as we are going to be in a Mortal Kombat like fighting tournament. Each season will be varying in what the main topic is and what characters we play. We may also switch DM's every season just to keep it extra spicy.

The goal of this podcast is to allow our audience an escape from reality. A time where they can truly relax and live in this fantasy world with us. A time where they can pretend they are in this fantasy world as one of our characters. We hope that this podcast will help people relax and put a smile on their faces.
